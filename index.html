Research

The focus of my current research is on supersymmetry in models of particles. Some predictions of superstring theory are in fact true already for particles (and hence do not rely on the conformal structure). I am deploying tools of homotopy such as BRST cohomology of these systems. In the past I have been questioning the target space geometry of some bosonic sigma models through higher structures, embodied in algebroids.
